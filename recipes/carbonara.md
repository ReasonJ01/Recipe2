## Ingredients
- [ ] 200g spaghetti
- [ ] 100g pancetta
- [ ] 2 large eggs
- [ ] 50g pecorino, grated
- [ ] Black pepper

## Method
1. Boil **200g spaghetti** in salted water for **10 minutes**. [timer:10m]
2. Fry **100g pancetta** for **5 minutes**. [timer:5m]
3. Whisk **2 eggs** with **50g pecorino**.
4. Combine pasta and pancetta off heat, then stir in egg mixture.

## Notes
- Use pasta water to loosen sauce.
- Grind pepper fresh at the end.
